About us

Edmira's Dance Academy is where dancers can learn from some of the leading choreographers in New Zealand. We are a Hip Hop Dance studio, catering for all ages and all levels of Hip Hop dance.The Palace Dance Studio is where you can learn, rehearse, create, collaborate and grow.Our studio promotes a supportive, smoke-free, alcohol-free, no swearing, friendly environment.The most important goal of The Palace is for each dancer to use Hip Hop Dance as a way to follow their dreams from dancing for fun to dancing around the world.Time to take your throne at The Palace Dance Studio!
